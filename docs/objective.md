# Project Objective

SocialPulse Market Intelligence aims to develop a social media analytics solution that collects, processes, and analyzes public social media data from multiple platforms. The solution will identify emerging trends, audience sentiment, and engagement patterns to generate actionable insights for marketing teams.

By leveraging user-generated content from platforms such as Reddit and YouTube, the project seeks to help marketing professionals better understand market interests, discover potential business opportunities, and support data-driven marketing and business development decisions.

The initial data collection will focus on technology and digital-business related discussions due to their high social media activity and relevance to marketing and business growth. Specific domains, topics, and trends will be refined throughout the project based on insights discovered during exploratory analysis.
