# Business Problem

Marketing teams rely on understanding customer interests, emerging trends, and audience engagement to make strategic decisions. However, social media platforms generate massive volumes of user-generated content, making it difficult to manually monitor discussions and extract meaningful insights.

As a result, organizations may miss emerging opportunities, changing customer interests, and market signals that could influence marketing strategies and business growth.

A scalable social media analytics solution is needed to collect, process, and analyze social media discussions and transform them into actionable business intelligence.
